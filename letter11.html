Letter Eleven

I don’t know what the future holds for us. I don’t know how time will change us, what life will ask of us, or how difficult some days might become. And sometimes that uncertainty scares me more than I admit.

There are moments when I wonder if one day life will pull you in a direction where choosing me becomes hard. And if that day ever comes, I want you to know this truth without any pressure or blame — I will still choose you.

Not because it’s easy. Not because it doesn’t hurt. But because loving you was never a decision I made lightly or carelessly. It was something my heart chose long before my mind could keep up.

Even if loving you means loving you quietly, from a distance, or in ways that don’t always look perfect, my choice will remain the same. You will always be someone I choose, with honesty, with respect, and with love.
